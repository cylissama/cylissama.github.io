About Me

I am Cy Dixon, a Computer Science student at Western Kentucky University, pursuing a BS in Computer Science with a concentration in System/Scientific Applications (ABET-accredited). My professional focus lies in exploring the intersections of computer science and engineering, particularly in web development, backend services, software engineering, and networking.

With hands-on experience in computer vision, robotics, and climate data analysis, I have developed robust programming skills in Python, MATLAB, Java, and C. Through my roles at the WKU Center for Energy Systems and the Kentucky Mesonet, I have gained expertise in real-world problem solving and data-driven projects. I am driven by curiosity and a passion for leveraging technology to solve practical problems.
